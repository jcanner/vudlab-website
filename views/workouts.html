Workouts

Motivation

Guitar players don't think, "Okay, put this finger here, and that finger there..." The little things are automatic. Likewise, data vis will be frustrating until low-level tasks are effortless. So, we made these 'workouts' to train your brain. Workouts build off the Dot Enter and Dot Append series by enjalot.

Theory

The workouts replicate how humans build muscle:

lift something outside comfort zone --> strain muscle --> eat protein --> rest --> muscle rebuilds stronger

try to code from memory --> confuse brain --> look how it's done --> rest --> brain rewires to automate task
