Workouts

Motivation

Guitar players don't think, "Now I put this finger here, and that finger there." The little things are automatic. Likewise, data vis will be frustrating until low-level tasks are automatic. So, we made these 'workouts' to code patterns into your brain. The whole series is based on the Dot Enter and Dot Append of tutorial series by enjalot.

Theory

The workouts imitate how humans build muscles:

lift something outside comfort zone --> strain muscle --> eat protein --> rest --> muscle rebuilds muscle stronger

try to code something from memory --> get confused --> look how it's done --> rest --> brain rewires to automate task

Instructions

.forEach...
  1. Look at the Target.
  2. Watch the Video (if there is one).
  3. Type the Answer Code line-by-line in your own tributary inlet (which you can make here.)
  4. Walk away. Clear your mind.
  5. Starting from the Setup Code, try to approximately remake the Target from memory.
  6. Stuck? Peek at the Answer Code.
  7. Repeat steps 4-6 until you've mastered the target.
